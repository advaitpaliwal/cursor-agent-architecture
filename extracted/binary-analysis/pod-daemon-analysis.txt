## Role
PID 1 in the container. Manages process lifecycle.
Creates/attaches to processes (like exec-daemon, cursorsandbox).
Streams process output events back to the orchestrator.
The orchestrator (vm-orchestrator) calls CreateProcess to start
exec-daemon, then AttachProcess to stream its stdout/stderr.
